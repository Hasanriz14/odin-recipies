Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs (usually chicken eggs)
stirred,whipped or beaten together while being gently heated
typically with salt,butter or oil and sometimes other ingredients


ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 7 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Process

  1. Step 1:
    Beat eggs in a bowl with a fork. Add milk, salt, and pepper; beat until well mixed.
  2. Step 2:
    Coat a small skillet with olive oil and heat over medium heat. Pour egg mixture into hot skillet;
    cook and stir until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes.
